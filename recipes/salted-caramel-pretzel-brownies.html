Salted Caramel Pretzel Brownies

Treat yourself to these dense and fudgy chocolate brownies, swirled with salted caramel and topped with pretzels.

Ingredients

    Stalted Caramel

  • 3/4 cup - sugar
  • 1/2 cup - heavy cream
  • 4 tbsp - unsalted butter room temperature
  • 1 to 2 tsp - sea salt to taste

    Brownies

  • 3/4 cup - unsalted butter cubed
  • 6 oz - bittersweet chocolate finely chopped
  • 1 cup - granulated sugar
  • 3 - eggs
  • 2 tbsp - cocoa powder preferably dark
  • 1 cup - all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp - salt
  • 1/2 cup - pretzels crushed into big pieces

Instructions

    Salted Caramel

  1. Place sugar in a large sauce pan on medium heat. Let the sugar disolve, with minimal stirring
  2. Once dissolved stop strirring and let the suger cook until its a deep amber color. [The sugar can burn very quickly if you aren't paying attention]
  3. Remove from heat and immediatly add the cubed butter and whisk constantly until the butter is melted
  4. While whisking, slowly pour in the heavy cream. Continue to whisk until the caramel in smooth
  5. Add a tsp of salt, add additional salt to desired taste
  6. Allow the caramel to chill in the refrigerator for 1 hour

    Brownies

  1. Preheat oven to 350 and line a 8x8 pan with parchment paper
  2. Combine butter and chocolate in a meduim saucepan over low, stir often until melted
  3. Add sugar and stir until combined
  4. Add eegs and stir to combine
  5. Add cocoa powder, flour, and salt. Stir the batch until the streak of flour disappear
  6. Pour the brownie batter into the the prepated pan. Dollop 1/2 cup of the salted caramel and use a knife to swirl into the brownie. Sprinkle the pretzels on top
  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes until its set in the middle and and done on the sides. A toothpick inserted contain mostly crumbs
  8. Tranfer the pan to a wire rack to cool off. Once cooled, remove brownies from the pan and drizzle the remaining caramel on top. Cut into squares
