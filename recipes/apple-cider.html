Apple Cider

Warm up your chilly nights with a warm spiced apple cider.

Ingredients

  • 10 - large apples cut into quarters
  • 1 - orange cut into half
  • 4 - cinnamon sticks
  • 1 tsp - whole cloves
  • 1 tsp - whole allspice
  • 1 - whole nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup - brown sugar, packed

Instructions

  1. In a large stock pot add the following:
    • apples
    • oranges
    • cinnamon sticks
    • cloves
    • allspice
    • nutmeg
    • brown sugar
  2. The the stockpot with water, until its about 2 inches above the ingredients. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to a simmer for 2 hours while covered
  3. remove the halves, then use a potatoe smasher or wooden spoon to mash the apples. Return to a simmer for 1 hours, leaving the pot uncovered
  4. Strain through a fine mesh stainer. Squeeze out the juice from the solids with a wooden spoon
  5. Serve the apple cider warm.
