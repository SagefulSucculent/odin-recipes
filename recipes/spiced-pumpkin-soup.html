Spiced Pumpkin Soup

A warm spicy pumpkin soup to fill both the stomach and heart.

Treat yourself this spooky season.

  • 1 pumpkin
  • 1 tbsp graram masala
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 - 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger, peeled and finely chopped
  • 2 garlics cloves, finely chopped
  • 900ml vegetables stock
  • 100ml double cream

    Step 1: Prep pumpkin

  1. preheat the oven to 180c
  2. cut the pumpkin in half
  3. scoop out the seeds
  4. continue to cut the pumpkin into wedgies
  5. prep the spices
    1. place the following ingredients into a small:
      • graram masala
      • 1 tsp coriander
      • 1 tsp cumin
    2. slowly pour 2 tbsp of olive oil, while mixing
  6. drizzle the spiced oil over the pumpkin
  7. tranfer ingredients to a baking tray and roast for 40-45 mins
  8. flip the pumpkin halfway through
  9. pumpkin is finshed once tender

    Step 2: Prep the stock

  1. heat 1 tbsp of olive oil in a large sauce pan
  2. fry the chopped onion until its becomes soft
  3. season with the following ingredients
    • pinch of salt
    • ginger
    • garlic
    • chilli flakes
    • 1 tsp coriander
    • 1 tsp cumin
  4. continue to fry until fragrant
  5. pour in the stock and bring to a simmer

    Step 3: The mixture

  1. spoon out the soft, cooled flesh from pumpkim
  2. add the flesh into the stock and remove it from the heat
  3. use a hand blander and blend the soup until its a smooth and creamy texture
  4. add any adtional seasoning according to your taste
  5. return the soup to low heat and stir the in the cream
  6. top with toasted pumpkin seeds
